Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Overview

GameNest (including mini-sites like GameNest Dice) is a casual web-gaming platform. We minimize data collection and focus on fast, fair, and fun gameplay.

What We Collect

  • Local storage: high scores, simple settings (kept in your browser only).
  • Contact messages: info you submit via forms (used to reply and support you).
  • Server logs (basic): temporary IP, user-agent, and error logs for security & debugging.

Cookies

We don’t set cookies by default. If we add analytics/ads later, those providers may use cookies under their own policies. We’ll update this page if our usage changes.

Analytics (Optional)

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand performance and fix issues. Data is aggregated and not used for profiling individual players.

Ads & Affiliates (Optional)

If we display ads or affiliate links, they’ll be clearly labeled. Third-party partners may process data according to their policies. We encourage you to review those policies as needed.

Your Choices

  • Clear site data in your browser to remove local scores/preferences.
  • Use private browsing to avoid storing local data.
  • Contact us to request deletion of messages you’ve submitted.

Children’s Privacy

GameNest is intended for general audiences. If you believe a minor has provided personal information, please contact us and we’ll address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. We’ll revise the “Effective date” above when updates are published.

Contact

Questions or requests? Please reach us via the Contact page.
